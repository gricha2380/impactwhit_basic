What We Do

The mission of ImpactWhit is to empower under-represented communities through technology and innovation. ImpactWhit creates a bridge for individuals in all stages of their career by incubating talent, capital, community and partnerships.

About Us

We are a registered 501(c)(3) organization established in 2020. We are a multi-disciplinary collective with individuals who are seeking to provide resources to each other so we can pass it off to the next generation. We are ensuring succession for a group of persons dedicated to changing the world positively. Through this we aim to eliminate 3rd world, accomplish sustainable development goals in order to get our communities to have the baseline.

Areas of Focus

  • Career Development

  • Finances
  • Personal Development
