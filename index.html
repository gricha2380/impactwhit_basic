What We Do

The mission of ImpactWhit is to empower under-represented communities through technology and innovation. ImpactWhit creates a bridge for individuals in all stages of their career by incubating talent, capital, community and partnerships.

  • Learn More

About Us

We are a registered 501(c)(3) organization established in 2020. We are a multi-disciplinary collective with individuals who are seeking to provide resources to each other so we can pass it off to the next generation. Ensuring succession for a group of persons dedicated to changing the world positively. Through this we can eliminate 3rd world, Sustainable Development Goals, getting our communities to have the baseline. Removal of crab in the bucket mentality.

    Community Membership

    • Must be 18 years of age
    • Must be actively learning (university, bootcamp, edTech platforms) or actively working in a technical, digital or innovative space (as an employee or entrepreneur)
    • Must have high ambitions around financial freedom and being involved in impactful creations
    • Must be located in the Caribbean, USA, Africa or be a part of an African diaspora
